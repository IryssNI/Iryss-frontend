# IRYSS · Outreach Playbook

> Purpose: book 10 demos with independent UK/Irish optician practice owners.
> Voice: professional peer, not vendor. Specific over clever. Short over polished.

---

## 1 · Who to target first

Single-site or 2–3-site UK/Irish independents where the owner is hands-on:

- **Tier 1** (book first): owner-operator, 1 practice, 1,000–4,000 patients, under 40, active on LinkedIn, mentioned the word "retention" or "Specsavers" in any post in the last 6 months.
- **Tier 2**: small groups (2–5 practices), AOP/ABDO members, independent CL specialists, myopia clinic operators.
- **Tier 3**: LOC chairs, industry commentators — not customers, but amplifiers.

**Do not target** (yet): multiples (Specsavers, Boots, Vision Express), hospital clinics, dispensing-only.

Source list: AOP directory, LinkedIn Sales Navigator filter ("optician" + "independent" + UK/IE), local LOC rosters, Instagram hashtag #independentoptician.

---

## 2 · The hook (30-word pitch)

> *Independent practices lose 20–30% of patients they should retain — because paper recalls die, SMS gets ignored, and CRMs weren't built to act on data. Iryss is the AI retention layer that sits alongside your CRM and wins them back over WhatsApp.*

Memorise it. Every touchpoint opens with a version of this.

## 6 · Phone / cold-call script (use for referred leads only)

**Opening (15 seconds):**

> Hi {FirstName}, it's {YourName} from Iryss — {Referrer} suggested I give you a call. Is this a bad time? … Great, I'll be quick.

**The hook (20 seconds):**

> Iryss is a retention layer that sits alongside your existing practice management system. We re-engage lapsed patients automatically over WhatsApp — 98% open rate vs 19% for SMS. The average independent practice we talk to is leaking £3–8k of patient revenue a month because recalls aren't getting through.

**The ask (10 seconds):**

> Would you have 15 minutes this week for a live demo? I'll pull up numbers from your own list — you'll see exactly what we'd recover for you.

**If yes:** Send calendar link on the call, confirm email, hang up.

**If "tell me more":** Ask two discovery questions (below), then ask for the meeting.

**If "not interested":** Thank them, ask if you can send a 2-minute video, hang up.

---

## 7 · Discovery questions (use on any call)

Pick 3–4. Don't interrogate.

1. *How many patients does the practice have on the books right now?*
2. *What's your average patient's lifetime value? Roughly?*
3. *How are you currently recalling patients? Letter, SMS, phone?*
4. *Of the patients who were due in the last 12 months, roughly what percentage rebooked?*
5. *What happens today when a patient doesn't turn up? Who chases?*
6. *Are you running a myopia clinic? If yes, how are you tracking axial length progression?*
7. *What CRM/PMS are you using? Happy with it?*
8. *If you had a magic wand for one part of your practice, what would it do?*

## 8 · Top 5 objections + answers

| Objection | Answer |
|-----------|--------|
| *"I already have a CRM — I don't need another tool."* | Exactly — Iryss isn't a replacement. Your CRM stores records. Iryss stops patients leaving. It sits alongside whatever you're using (Optix, Ocuco, etc.) — no migration, no retraining. |
| *"£199/month is expensive."* | Average patient lifetime value is £800–1,200. If Iryss retains 2 extra patients a month, that's £20k/year. Your investment is £2,400. The maths speaks for itself. |
| *"My patients are older — they don't use WhatsApp."* | 30 million UK adults use WhatsApp monthly. 73% message brands daily. Even patients over 60 have a 68% WhatsApp open rate vs 14% SMS. We can still SMS the rest — you're not picking one. |
| *"I don't have time for another login."* | You open it once a day for 2 minutes — it's designed to tell you "today you need to do these 3 things." Not a data-entry tool. The time saving on recall admin alone is ~4 hours a week. |
| *"Data security — is it GDPR compliant?"* | Fully. UK data servers, encryption at rest and in transit, per-practice data isolation, GOC-ready audit logs. Happy to send our security one-pager. |

## 9 · Demo structure (15-min meeting)

1. **2 min** · their world: 3 discovery questions max
2. **8 min** · Iryss walkthrough in this order:
   - Dashboard: Practice Score + "Where you're losing money" card (use their data if you have it)
   - Click a leakage row → launch Campaign Builder → live personalised send
   - Inbox: show the replies landing
   - Myopia Clinic (if relevant) or AI Scribe (wow moment)
3. **3 min** · pricing + onboarding logistics
4. **2 min** · ask for the sale: "Would you like to start on a 30-day free trial?"

Don't demo every feature. Demo the 3 they reacted to most.

## 12 · Metrics to hit

Per 100 cold emails, expect:
- 25–35 opens
- 5–8 replies
- 2–3 booked demos
- 0.5–1 closed customer

**So: 10 demos = 400 well-targeted outbound touches.** Plan accordingly.
