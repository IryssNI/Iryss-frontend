Three questions every optician asks us

Here are the honest answers.

🔍

What does Iryss do?

Iryss plugs into your existing CRM and watches for patients slipping away — overdue recalls, lapsed contact-lens wearers, missed appointments. It automatically re-engages them via WhatsApp before they walk into a competitor. It gives you real-time revenue calculators so you can see exactly how much you're recovering. And it includes a dedicated Myopia Clinic — axial length tracking, treatment plans, parent WhatsApp, AI recommendations — all in one place.

A retention engine, revenue tracker & myopia clinic in one
💡

What problem does it solve?

You're losing patients you shouldn't be. Paper recalls go in the bin. SMS gets ignored. Your CRM stores data — it doesn't act on it. Iryss turns that data into real conversations that bring patients back. Our revenue dashboards show you exactly what's at risk, what's been recovered, and what's left on the table — so every decision is backed by numbers, not guesswork.

Practices lose 20–30% of patients to inaction
⚡

How easy is it to set up?

You don't replace anything. Iryss connects alongside your existing system — Optix, Ocuco, Acuitas, Optisoft, XEYEX, or whatever you use. No data migration, no staff retraining, no downtime. Your team keeps working exactly as they do now — Iryss just works quietly in the background, recovering patients and revenue.
