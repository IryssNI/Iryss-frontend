## Recording Tips

1. **Voice tone:** Confident but warm. You're showing a friend something brilliant, not reading a corporate script.
2. **Pace:** Match the captions. They're timed to guide you.
3. **Emphasis words:** "142 patients", "twelve thousand pounds", "42 percent", "eighteen thousand pounds" — these are your power numbers.
4. **Screen recording:** 1280×720 minimum. Chrome fullscreen (F11) looks cleanest.
5. **Software:** OBS Studio (free) or QuickTime (Mac) both work well.
6. **Audio:** Use a decent mic close to your mouth. Even AirPods sound better than a laptop mic.
7. **Post-production:** Trim the start/end, add subtle background music (try artlist.io for royalty-free tracks), export as MP4.
