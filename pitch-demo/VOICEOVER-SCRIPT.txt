=====================================
RECORDING TIPS
=====================================

1. Voice: Confident but warm, like you're showing
   a friend something brilliant.
2. Pace: Match the captions on screen.
3. Power numbers: "67 patients", "four thousand
   pounds", "42 percent", "hundred thousand a year"
   — lean into these.
4. Screen record: Cmd+Shift+5 on Mac.
5. Audio: AirPods work, but a decent mic is better.
6. Post: Trim start/end, add subtle background
   music (artlist.io), export as MP4.


=====================================
FIGURES RATIONALE (for your reference)
=====================================

These numbers reflect a typical single-site
independent optician in the UK/NI/ROI:

- 3,842 patients on the books
  (typical indie has 3,000-5,000)
- 67 at-risk patients
  (~10-15% lapse rate is industry norm, shown
   as monthly overdue cohort)
- 18 recalls per day
  (practice doing 8-10 sight tests/day)
- £4.2k revenue at risk per month
  (67 patients x avg ~£160 per visit value
   including dispensing)
- 156 recalls per month
  (~7-8 per working day across SMS/email/WhatsApp)
- 53 appointments booked from recalls
  (34% response rate)
- £8.4k recovered per month
  (53 patients x avg £160 per visit)
- £100k+ per year
  (£8.4k x 12 = £100.8k annually)

Sources:
- GOC Mapping of Optical Businesses (2023)
- AOP Practice Owner surveys
- NHS England Digital sight test data
- Average UK independent turnover: £300k-500k
  (most independents in £100k-£500k band per GOC)
